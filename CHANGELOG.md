# Changelog

All notable changes to this document are recorded here. This project follows
the spirit of [Keep a Changelog](https://keepachangelog.com/) and uses simple
semantic versioning for a text document: the **major** number changes when the
Articles change in substance; the **minor** number changes for clarifications,
sources, or supporting files.

## [1.0] — 2026-06-12

The first public release.

### Added
- Preamble, seven foundational Articles, and the Final Principle — including the
  sharpened treatment of verification (Article 3), critical thinking and
  automation bias (Article 5), and the explicit naming of fairness and
  non-discrimination (Article 6).
- A short *Sources and Inspirations* section linking each Article to the
  governance and historical instruments that inform it (EU AI Act, EU Ethics
  Guidelines for Trustworthy AI, OECD AI Principles, Magna Carta, UN Charter,
  IBM decision-maker guidance).
- Repository scaffolding: README, CC BY 4.0 license notice, citation metadata,
  contributing guide, and this changelog.
- Streamlined to a single, one-page charter for clarity and ease of adoption.
