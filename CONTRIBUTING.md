# Contributing

Thank you for your interest in improving the Human–AI Collaboration Constitution.

## How to propose a change

- **Open an issue** to discuss a clarification, a new source, or a suggested
  revision before submitting a pull request. This keeps the document coherent
  and the history readable.
- **Pull requests** are welcome for typos, broken links, clearer wording, and
  additions to the *Sources and Inspirations* list.

## A note on the Articles

The seven Articles and the Final Principle are intentionally **stable**. Edits
that change their substance are weighed carefully and should preserve the
central commitment that responsibility for the work remains with a human author.
Proposals that would weaken or remove that commitment are out of scope.

## Style

- Keep the language plain, precise, and free of jargon where possible.
- Reference external instruments by name; do not paste third-party text into the
  repository.
- If you add a source, include a one-line note on which Article it supports.
